Amniotic fluid cfRNA

This site contains the analysis of RNAseq data generated from amniotic fluid cell-free RNA (cfRNA).

The data is from Lisa Hui in the Embryonic Epidemiology group at the Murdoch Children’s Research Institute (MCRI). They have collected amniotic fluid from pregnancies with suspected (and subsequently confirmed) cytomegalovirus (CMV) infection. They also have amniotic fluid from “normal” pregnancies i.e. pregnancies that were confirmed to be negative for CMV infection. They are interested in differences in the cfRNA between the CMV negative and positive pregnancies. Nineteen samples (10 CMV negative and 9 CMV positive) were originally sequenced (150bp paired-end) at the MCRI TGU. A further batch of 9 samples was then sequenced.

Follow the links below to explore the different parts of the analysis.

Differential gene expression (DGE) analysis
